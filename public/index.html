Susie Meyers PGA Golf Instructor

Golf Digest Top 100 professional golf instructor based in Tucson, AZ.

Susie's approach to spending time with a student is based upon the philosophies of Point A Golf. It is flexible instruction geared to the differences found in every student’s style. Over the years Susie's clients have included a large volume of men, women and juniors, state champions, PGA Tour winners, LPGA players, recreational players and beginners. Many golfers have said they find it to be a very unique and insightful time. This view is built upon years of competitive golf and 30 years of coaching. Susie's mission is to provide and support a positive learning experience.

Susie has been a member of The PGA of America since 1994, competed on the LPGA Tour, played in 4 US Opens and one LPGA Championship.
