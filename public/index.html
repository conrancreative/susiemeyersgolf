Susie Meyers PGA Golf Instructor

Golf Magazine Top 100 professional golf instructor based in Tucson, AZ.

Have you ever been frustrated with your game; finding no progress and /or feeling confused? Or are you the player that wants to develop your game to its highest potential? Either way, time with Susie will surely benefit you. Developing a golf game is a process that takes understanding and a level of commitment. Susie has guided golfers of every age and ability for over 35 years. She was a player at the highest level before she began coaching and her past experiences along with her desire to continue learning have been the reasons so many have improved their games under her tutelage. She has coached two PGA Tour winners and many high-level golfers that are playing for their living. Many of her young students have gone to play collegiate golf on scholarship. She is passionate about helping the beginner and the recreational golfer because Susie just loves helping people!

Susie has been a member of The PGA of America since 1994, competed on the LPGA Tour, played in 4 US Opens and one LPGA Championship.
