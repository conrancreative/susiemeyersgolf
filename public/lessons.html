Lessons & Packages

All lessons Take Place Ventana Canyon Golf and Racquet Club in Tucson Arizona.

People tell me I teach very differently. I am not sure why but I do know I get different results than I used to when I used video analysis, positions and focusing on the problems. I like to work with pictures and ideas of what To Do instead of finding what is wrong in a person’s golf stroke. If a coach can change things in someone’s golf stroke without them even knowing what is wrong then that is a qualified instructor.

My goal is to work on what a golfer wants to do and have the natural system of how the body and mind work together clean up a stroke without the player even knowing what is wrong. I would rather a player think about the move he wants to make and work on the positive move hopefully without even having the player know that there is a problem.

Lesson Details

Standard Lesson

1 Hour

Overview:

Students are presented with the Point A philosophy Susie will work to find out what the student wants and improve that part of the game.

Extended Lesson

2 Hours

Overview:

With more time, Susie can explain the Point A philosophy more deeply. Susie will work on what part of the game the student wants to improve and a clear understanding of the golf clubs (tools) and how to use them.

Half Day Package

4 Hours
Free Book Inlcuded

Overview:

Susie will work on each part of the game in-depth from the mental game, short game, and long game.

Full Day Package

6 Hours
Free Book Inlcuded

Overview:

This is best done after some previous lesson time with Susie. The days consists of various range sessions and one hour on the course. Susie will address mental game, course strategies and shots around the greens.
